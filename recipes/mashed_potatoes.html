Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potato or mashed potatoes (American and Canadian English), colloquially known as mash (British English),is a dish made by mashing boiled or steamed potatoes, usually with added milk, butter, salt and pepper. It is generally served as a side dish to meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cream Cheese
  • Fine sea salt

Steps to prepare

  1. Cut the potatoes
  2. Boil the potatoes
  3. Prepare your melted butter mixture
  4. Pan-dry the potatoes
  5. Mash the potatoes
  6. Stir everything together
  7. Taste and season
  8. Serve warm
